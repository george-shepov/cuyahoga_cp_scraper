Will I Lose My License After an Ohio DUI?

License risk is one of the first things people worry about after an OVI arrest, and for good reason.

Why license risk feels immediate

People often feel the impact of an OVI before the rest of the case is even underway because driving is tied to work, school, and family obligations.

  • Work commute
  • Medical appointments
  • Childcare and family needs

What affects the answer

The answer depends on the charge pattern and what happened with any chemical test.

  • Refusal or completed test
  • Prior history
  • Commercial driving issues
